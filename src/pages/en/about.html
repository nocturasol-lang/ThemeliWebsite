About

THEMELI S.A. is a Greek engineering and construction company with a long-standing and dynamic presence in the sector of large-scale infrastructure projects.

With its origins dating back to 1923, and with the construction of buildings that are now considered landmarks of Greece, the company has maintained uninterrupted operations up to the present day. Its activities include both public and private projects, as well as infrastructure works such as motorways, tunnels, bridges, wind farms, railway projects, sewage networks, buildings, and more.

In its current form, the company was founded on December 31, 1986, by Dimitrios Dinopoulos, who serves as Chairman of the Board, while Georgios Dinopoulos holds the position of Chief Executive Officer (CEO).

THEMELI S.A. operates with certified management systems for quality, environmental management, energy management, and occupational health and safety. The company undertakes major infrastructure projects both in Greece and internationally, either independently or through joint ventures. This capability is supported by high-specification equipment, strong financial capacity, modern organization, and highly specialized personnel.

THEMELI S.A., officially Themeli Anonymous Technical Company, is headquartered in Nea Ionia, Attica (104 Kapodistriou Street) and serves as the main company of a group that includes the following subsidiaries, covering a wide range of business activities:

  • THERMIS S.A. – Active in the field of renewable energy production, primarily through wind farms, with the following Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) subsidiaries:
    • XIROVOUNI S.A. – 17 MW Wind Farm in operation since 2013
    • PERGANTI S.A. – 41.8 MW Wind Farm in operation since 2020
  • THEMOS S.A. – Specializes in the production of precast concrete elements, such as prestressed railway sleepers and spun concrete poles.
  • Tetrapolis Keos I.K.E. – Active in the tourism and hospitality sector, as well as in real estate development and management.
