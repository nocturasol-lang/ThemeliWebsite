About

Themeli is the oldest construction company in Greece and executes major technical works (motorways, tunnels, bridges, railroad projects, drainage networks, buildings e.t.c.), public or private, individually or in joint-venture with other European companies. Nowadays, with excellent mechanical equipment, high quality infrastructure, modern organization and key personnel with experience and commitment, themeli, continues its course in any kind of public or private projects.

Themeli achieves high quality construction projects, according to the requirements of modern technology and design, and their timely completion and delivery.
