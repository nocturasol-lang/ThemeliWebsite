About

THEMELI S.A. is a Greek engineering and construction company with a long-standing and dynamic presence in the sector of large-scale infrastructure projects.

With its origins dating back to 1923, and with the construction of buildings that are now considered landmarks of Greece, the company has maintained uninterrupted operations up to the present day. Its activities include both public and private projects, as well as infrastructure works such as motorways, tunnels, bridges, wind farms, railway projects, sewage networks, buildings, and more.

In its current form, the company was founded on December 31, 1986, by Dimitrios Dinopoulos, who serves as Chairman of the Board, while Georgios Dinopoulos holds the position of Chief Executive Officer (CEO).

THEMELI S.A. operates with certified management systems for quality, environmental management, energy management, and occupational health and safety. The company undertakes major infrastructure projects both in Greece and internationally, either independently or through joint ventures. This capability is supported by high-specification equipment, strong financial capacity, modern organization, and highly specialized personnel.

THEMELI S.A., officially Themeli Anonymous Technical Company, is headquartered in Nea Ionia, Attica (104 Kapodistriou Street) and serves as the main company of a group that includes the following subsidiaries, covering a wide range of business activities:

  • THERMIS S.A. – Active in the field of renewable energy production, primarily through wind farms, and holding the following special purpose subsidiaries.
    • XIROVOUNI S.A. – Wind power plant (17 MW) in operation since 2013.
    • PERGANTI S.A. – Wind power plant (41.8 MW) in operation since 2020.
  • THEMOS S.A. – Specializes in the production of precast concrete elements, such as prestressed railway sleepers and centrifuged concrete poles.
  • Tetrapolis Keos I.K.E. – Active in the tourism and hospitality sector, as well as real estate development and management.

History

The history of the modern company THEMELI S.A. is closely linked to one of the oldest and most pioneering engineering companies in Greece, ELLINIKA TECHNIKA ERGA S.A., which was founded in Athens in the previous century and distinguished itself through its significant contribution to public and private projects across the country. This historic company later merged with THEMELI S.A.

Specifically, ELLINIKA TECHNIKA ERGA S.A. was founded in 1923 by Periklis Triantafyllidis, a civil engineer graduate of the National Technical University of Athens, and became a major pillar of the construction sector in Greece for several decades, contributing significantly to the country's reconstruction and development.

During the interwar period, the company carried out numerous projects in cooperation with the Ministry of National Defense and the Hellenic Navy General Staff, including road construction works and the construction of many public and private buildings. Among these were hospitals such as KAT, Agios Savvas, and Aglaia Kyriakou, as well as the Army Share Fund building, the Palace Hotel, and the renovation of emblematic buildings such as the Athens Court of First Instance and the Bank of Athens building on Stadiou Street.

In the post-war period, the company actively participated in the reconstruction of Greece following the end of the civil war, undertaking major road projects such as the Thessaloniki–Turkish border highway, as well as important building projects including refineries in Aspropyrgos, industrial facilities, hospitals, universities, and more.

In 1995, the company merged with the newer but rapidly developing THEMELI S.A., which had been founded in 1986 by Dimitrios Dinopoulos.

Following the 1995 merger, the expertise and activities of the historic company were fully integrated into THEMELI S.A., which continued its dynamic growth, expanding its activities in infrastructure construction while also entering additional business sectors.
