About

THEMELI S.A. is a Greek engineering and construction company with a long-standing and dynamic presence in the large-scale infrastructure sector — its origins dating back to 1923.

Having delivered buildings that are now considered Greek landmarks, the company has maintained uninterrupted operations to this day, undertaking both public and private projects, including infrastructure works such as highways, tunnels, bridges, wind farms, railway projects, sewerage networks, buildings, and more.

In its current corporate form, the company was established on December 31, 1986, by Mr. Dimitrios Ntinopoulos, who serves as Chairman of the Board of Directors, while Mr. Georgios Ntinopoulos holds the position of Chief Executive Officer.

THEMELI S.A. operates certified management systems for Quality, Environmental Management, Energy Management, and Occupational Health & Safety. The company undertakes major infrastructure projects in Greece and abroad, either independently or through joint ventures, supported by high-specification equipment, strong financial capacity, modern organizational structure, and highly specialized personnel.

THEMELI S.A. is headquartered in Nea Ionia, Attica (104 Kapodistriou Street) and constitutes the parent company of a Group that includes the following subsidiaries.

Subsidiaries
  • THERMIS S.A. — active in the production of energy from Renewable Energy Sources (RES), primarily through wind farms
    • XIROVOUNI S.A. — 17 MW Wind Farm in operation since 2013
    • PERGANTI S.A. — 41.8 MW Wind Farm in operation since 2020
  • THEMOS S.A. — specializes in the production of precast concrete elements, such as prestressed railway sleepers and spun concrete poles.
  • Tetrapolis Keos I.K.E. — active in the tourism and hospitality sector, as well as in real estate development and management.
